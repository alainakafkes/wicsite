NU Women in Computing

We are Northwestern Women In Computing

NuWiC is an organization for women interested in computer science and related fields. We help to guide women academically and professionally, by hosting hack nights, interview prep and monthly lunches. We also organize a group to go to Grace Hopper, the premier female computing conference in the country.

Events

Each year we host and attend a variety of events. These include monthly lunches, general meetings, hackathons, interview prep and much more. See more about our annual events below, and check out our Facebook page for more the most current information.

Each year we attend Grace Hopper, a large conference celebrating women in computer sciences. This year, a group of almost 20 women traveled down to Texas for the proceedings.
